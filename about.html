Our Story


Our business is dedicated to providing high-class cakes, cupcakes sweet treats for those who love to indulge in sweetness! Using only the finest of ingredients, we ensure that your dessert needs will not only be met but surpassed!

We normally operate and sell our sugary goods in our centres based in the Chadstone and City areas here in Melbourne, Victoria but, as you all know, the current lockdowns have not made our face to face operations possible but this has not stopped us from reaching out to you as the collaboration with the Faithful Hippos Team has made this possible!
Feel free to explore, customise your items and eat to your heart's content.

We hope you enjoy our sweets just as much as we enjoy creating them for you.
Don't hesitate to comment, send an enquiry or contact us via our email or phone for any of your delicious questions.
